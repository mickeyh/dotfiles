* Linux configuration, intended for use with i3wm.
* Config also works OK with Windows (maybe someday I'll improve it). 

* Swap Esc and CapsLock for Vim.
[escape]>[caps]
[caps]>[escape]

* Left Mod/Win to inner thumb; backspace to delete; delete is orphaned.
[delete]>[bspace]
[bspace]>[lwin]

* Page Up/Down are shifted up by one; left Alt overwrites Page Down.
* This (combined with above) orphans left Ctrl but offers easy access to Alt.
[pdown]>[lalt]
[pup]>[pdown]
[rwin]>[pup]
