* OSX configuration, intended for use with Karabiner-Elements (to create Hyper)
* and Hammerspoon (to maniuplate windows).

* Swap Esc and CapsLock for Vim.
[escape]>[caps]
[caps]>[escape]

* Left Cmd/Win to inner thumb; backspace to delete; delete is orphaned.
[delete]>[bspace]
[bspace]>[lwin]

* Right Cmd/Win to right Ctrl since it's the dominant OS modifer key.
[rctrl]>[rwin]

* Page Up/Down are shifted up by one; left Alt overwrites Page Down.
* This (combined with above) orphans left Ctrl but offers easy access to Alt.
[pdown]>[lalt]
[pup]>[pdown]
[rwin]>[pup]
